Welcome to NeuroVault - a public web repository for statistical maps of the human brain. After filling in the minimum information outlined below (only the fields with an asterisk [*] are compulsory), your map will be sent back to Neurosynth.org for decoding. Remember that your map needs to be unthresholded and in MNI152 space for decoding to work correctly!. By uploading your map you agree to make it publicly available under CC0 license. You can always come back to NeuroVault.org to inspect, modify, and/or delete your maps. If you wish to use Neurosynth decoding, but keep your maps private, consider creating a a private collection.
